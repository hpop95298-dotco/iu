Attendance

Students must attend at least 75% of the total contact hours for every course (lectures and tutorials/labs).

Failure to meet this requirement may lead to the student being barred from final examinations.

Declaration of Honor

To achieve the declaration of honor, a student must maintain a cumulative GPA of 3.00 or higher throughout their study.

Requirements: No failures in any course and completion within 8 semesters.

Credit Hour Load

  • Academic Probation: Max 12 hours if GPA < 2.0.
