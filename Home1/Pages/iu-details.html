Our Vision

The Faculty of Computers and Information Technology shall achieve excellence in the fields of information systems, computer science, artificial intelligence, and digital informatics, and that we be creative in developing the learning journey for students who are the focus of the educational process, and that we have leadership in our specialty at the local and regional levels.

Our Mission

The Faculty is committed to providing students with basic knowledge and skills and providing an educational environment conducive to innovation in the field of computers and digital informatics with a balance between the ethical, professional, technical, and scientific aspects, and graduating highly competitive professionals and to contribute to advancing technological development in all its aspects for Egypt.

Strategic Objectives

Developing competitiveness through an advanced educational system that suits rapid growth in IT fields.
Developing scientific creativity to keep pace with modern scientific trends in specialization.
Developing innovation and creativity skills for our students.
Providing a standard environment and a typical climate for the educational process.
Developing practical creativity to keep pace with the requirements of specialized institutions.
Developing communication and teamwork skills.
Developing business management and leadership skills.
Developing students’ awareness of ethical standards.
The ability to self-learn for life.
The ability of dynamism, agility, resilience, and flexibility.

Academic Path Overview

Foundation (Level 1)

A general foundation year shared across programs to build core logic and technical skills.

  • Intro to Programming & OOP
  • Math, Physics & Logic
  • Professional English & Soft Skills

Specialization (Levels 2-3)

Deep diving into specific program tracks and master core computing technologies.

  • Data Structures & DBMS
  • OS & Computer Networks
  • Web & Mobile Development

Professional Exit (Level 4)

Advanced electives and industry-ready projects to prepare for the global market.

  • AI, Cloud & Cybersecurity
  • Specialized Program Electives
  • Capstone Project (1 & 2)
